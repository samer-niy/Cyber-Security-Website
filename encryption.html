Encryption

Symmetric Encryption

Symmetric encryption is a method of encrypting plaintext using a single, shared secret key. It is also known as private key encryption.

Asymmetric Encryption

Asymmetric encryption is a method of encrypting plaintext using a pair of public and private keys. It is also known as public key encryption.
